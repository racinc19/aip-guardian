# AIP Mission Document

  ## Mission Statement

  AIP (Artificial Intelligence Protocol) is the overarching mission framework. Guardian is the first executable asset deployed in service of this mission.

  ## Objectives

  1. **Deploy Guardian** - Establish the first executable presence
  2. **Maintain Integrity** - Ensure all assets remain operationally sound
  3. **Expand Reach** - Scale the mission framework as needed
  4. **Document Progress** - Track all developments transparently

  ## Guardian Role

  Guardian serves as the primary entry point and operational asset for the AIP mission. Its responsibilities include:

  - Acting as the public-facing deployment
  - Providing the foundation for future AIP assets
  - Demonstrating the mission's operational capability
